Long-term Reliability

This 2018 Honda Accord EX-L generally shows above-average reliability with proper maintenance. Most units reach 150,000 miles with only minor issues, though transmission concerns may arise in higher mileage examples.

The 1.5L turbocharged engine is relatively new to the Accord lineup, but has proven reliable across Honda's vehicle range with proper oil change intervals. The CVT transmission has improved significantly over previous generations, though some owners report hesitation or "rubber band" feeling under certain driving conditions.

Electrical systems are generally reliable, with the infotainment system receiving occasional software updates to address minor bugs.

Performance Analysis

This vehicle offers balanced performance with adequate power for most driving scenarios. The 1.5L turbocharged engine provides 192 horsepower, which delivers responsive acceleration in daily driving conditions. Turbo lag is minimal, with peak torque available at low RPMs (1,600 rpm).

Handling is predictable though not sporty, with comfort-oriented suspension tuning that prioritizes ride quality over cornering ability. The Accord features a MacPherson strut front suspension and multi-link rear setup that delivers a composed ride over various road surfaces.

The CVT transmission is tuned for efficiency but includes simulated gear changes under heavy acceleration to provide a more natural driving feel compared to earlier CVT designs.
